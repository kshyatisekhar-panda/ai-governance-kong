Atlas Copco Case Management

Customer service portal with governed AI triage. Every AI call routes through Kong and the governance gateway.

Modules

Customer Chat

Free-text customer support. PII (email, phone) masked before reaching the LLM. Sensitive IDs and bulk-PII keywords blocked at the gateway.

Create Case

Structured CRM intake form. Raw PII stored in the case record; only redacted operational context is sent to the LLM for AI triage.

Case Inbox

Agent view of open and recent cases. Filter by status, priority, AI triage state. Drill into any case to view detail and run actions.

AI Triage & Draft Reply

Per-case AI summary and suggested first response, generated from a redacted context. Re-run triage or draft a new reply on demand.

How it works

  1. Customer or agent submits input via one of the modules above.
  2. Request hits Kong on port 8000 — key-auth, rate-limit, and (for chat) ai-prompt-guard regex deny patterns fire here.
  3. Kong forwards to the governance gateway (Express). PII is scanned per app policy: email/phone masked, SSN/credit card/personnummer blocked.
  4. Cases are persisted with raw PII in service_cases; only redacted context is sent to the LLM for triage.
  5. Every request is audited in request_logs with team, app, model, cost, latency, and decision.
