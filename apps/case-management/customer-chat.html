Customer Chat

Free-text customer support. Goes through Kong's ai-prompt-guard (regex deny patterns), then the governance gateway's PII scanner, smart router, budget check, and audit log.

Try: "Hi, I'm Lars from Nordic Mining. My GA 75 compressor stopped twice today, please call me at +46 70 123 45 67." — phone will be masked. Try a Swedish personnummer or SSN to see Kong block at the edge.

All requests use API key case-key-2024 (Customer Service team / Case Management app). Audit rows show up in the dashboard's Governance Audit page.
